## Q: How do I connect to Miners Online?

For detailed instructions on connecting to Miners Online, please refer to our [Getting Started guide](getting-started.md).

## Q: What Minecraft versions are supported?

For information on supported Minecraft versions, please see the [Getting Started guide](getting-started.md#version-compatibility) under the "Version Compatibility" section.

## Q: Where can I find more resources about Miners Online?

You can find more resources at our:
- [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aeRReEaNnm)
- [GitHub organisation](https://github.com/miners-online)
- [Live Map](https://maps.minersonline.uk)

## Q: How can I report bugs or contribute to the project?

You can report bugs or contribute to Miners Online by visiting our [GitHub organisation](https://github.com/miners-online), or navigate the in-game links to the relevant repositories.

## Q: Who can I contact for support?

For support, you can join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aeRReEaNnm) where community members and staff are available to help.

## Q: Are there any rules I should be aware of?

Yes, please make sure to read and follow the server rules available at [Rules guide](rules.md) to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

## Q: Is there a way to view the server world online?

Yes, you can view the server world using our [Live Map](https://maps.minersonline.uk).

## Q: Can I play on Miners Online using a console?

Yes, you can play on Miners Online using a console by following [GeyserMC's instructions](https://geysermc.org/wiki/geyser/using-geyser-with-consoles/) for connecting Bedrock Edition clients.

## Q: How do I stay updated with the latest news about Miners Online?

To stay updated with the latest news about Miners Online, you can join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aeRReEaNnm) and follow our [website](https://minersonline.uk).

## Q: Are there any mods used on Miners Online?

Miners Online primarily runs a vanilla Minecraft experience with some server-side plugins for enhanced gameplay.

## Q: Can I suggest new features for Miners Online?

Yes, we welcome suggestions for new features! You can share your ideas on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aeRReEaNnm) or open an issue in our [GitHub organisation](https://github.com/miners-online).

## Q: Is there a way to donate to support Miners Online?

Not at the moment, but we appreciate your enthusiasm! Stay tuned for any future updates regarding donations.

## Q: Where can I find the server status?

Server status updates can be found on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/aeRReEaNnm) only.
